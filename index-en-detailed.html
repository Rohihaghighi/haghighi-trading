🧾 Types of Commerce

1. Domestic Trade: Includes the exchange of goods and services within a country’s borders.

2. International Trade: Includes the export and import of goods between countries.

🧭 Main Processes in Commerce:

  • Market research and identifying opportunities
  • Finding suppliers or customers
  • Negotiating and signing commercial contracts
  • Customs clearance and transportation
  • Cargo insurance and delivery tracking

🚢 Transportation Methods in Commerce:

  • Ground (Truck, Trailer, Train)
  • Sea (Container Ships)
  • Air (Cargo Planes)
  • Combined

🧰 Company Services:

  • Identifying target markets
  • Customs consultation and clearance
  • Secure packaging and transport
  • Quality assurance

🎯 Commercial Goals:

  • Increase profit and market share
  • Develop non-oil exports
  • Meet domestic needs through imports
  • Enhance company competitiveness
